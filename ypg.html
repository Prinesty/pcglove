YPG "Rep Your Jersey"

5th March 2023

Story

The Young Peoples Guild (YPG) of the Presbyterian Church of Ghana Love Congregation Oko marked the Launch of their annual Youth and Students Week (YSW) Celebration on Sunday the 5th of March 2023 with Rep your Jersey Sunday.

As part of the launch,YPG members as well as some church members of the entire Congregation where seen repping their favorite football club Jersey dancing and Glorifying God.

The resident Minister Rev. Dr Joseph Kofi AIDOO praised the Young Peoples guild for looking colourful as well as duly launching the Youth and Students Week Celebrations.

Week Activities
Wednesday 8th March Exposition on the Theme: Influenced by Christ, set for his glory.
Thursday 9th March: A symposium : Tales from the powder room.
Friday 10th March: Worship and Prayer Night.
Saturday 11th March: Congregational Sports activity between Love Congreghation, Calvary Congregation and Logos Congregation.
