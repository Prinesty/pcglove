Lilian Yeboah Wadie Block

On Sunday 9th March, 2025 the Lilian Yeboah Wadie Block was dedicated by Rev. Col. David Addoteye Asare (GA Presbytery Chairperson), assisted by Rev. Isaac Quist ( GA Presbytery Clerk) and Rev. Dr. Joseph Kofi Aidoo (Minister- In- Charge) in the name of the Truine God.

Other ministers and District reps present were Rev. Patrick Nii Owusu Armah (PCG Property Manager), Rev. Clifford Amane Sarpong (PCG, Salem Congregation), Mr. Meshach Asah-Kisseidu (Taifa District Lay Rep), Mr. Bernard Buertey Okutu (Taifa District Clerk).

This beautiful edifice was erected by Mrs. Lilian E. Yeboah Wadie & family to commiserate Mrs. Wadie's 70th Birthday and also to thank God for his marvelous blessings upon her life. The building entails Children's place of service, Brigade Office with instruments and washroom.

To God be the Glory!

Written by Mrs. Benedicta Adubea Quartey
