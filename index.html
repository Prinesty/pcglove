Visit to Mrs. Gifty Owusu Darko

In our quest to see our mothers, fathers, grandfathers and grandmothers with a smile on their faces, we begun with a support of bottled water to the aged on every aged Sunday Service in the year.
This gesture started about 6 years ago. In the second year, we decided to take it a step further.
We contributed some money as a group voluntarily to support the purchases of some groceries in order to share for them.
This has continued for the past 4 years and the Lord keeps strengthening us as a group. This continued act of sharing stems from the happiness we see on their faces.

Induction Service of Rev. Dr. J.K. Aidoo

The Induction Service of the Rev. Dr. Joseph Kofi Aidoo was held on the 14th of February, 2021 at the PCG Love Congregation Church.

Brief biography of Rev. Dr. Joseph Kofi Aidoo of the Presbyterian Church of Ghana, Love Congregation, New Legon (Oko)

Rev. Dr. Joseph Kofi Aidoo was born on the 1 st day of September 1989 He had his Secondary school education at Mfansipim Senior High School in the Central Region of Ghana.
From Mfansipim, he then proceeded to the University of Ghana Medical School where he completed in 2014. He began his profession at the Tamale Teaching Hospital and then moved to the Ghana Police Hospital and finally Madina Polyclinic. He is currently a resident Psychiatrist at the Ghana College of Physicians and Surgeons, Ridge. In 2015, he went to the Ramseyer Training Seminary at Abetifi to have his ministerial training. He was successfully commissioned in 2018.
Rev. Dr. Joseph Kofi Aidoo is married to Eugenia Sybil Nana Ama Aidoo and their marriage is blessed with a daughter.

“PERSEVERANCE”
“For at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up.” Galatians 6:9

Perseverance doesn't demand more than we have, but all that we have. Perseverance means more than just trying or working hard. Perseverance is an investment.
Its willingness to bind yourself emotionally, intellectually, physically, spiritually to an idea, purpose or task until it has been completed. Perseverance demands a lot, but here’s the good news: everything you give is an investment in yourself.
Each time you do the right thing seek God, work hard, treat others with respect, learn and grow, you invest in yourself. To do these things everyday takes perseverance. Dream only become a reality, when we keep our commitment to them.

Be Baptized by the Holy Spirit
"John answered them all, I baptize you with water. But one more powerful than I will come, the thongs of whose sandals I am not worthy to untie. He will baptize you with fire." Luke 3:16

From the opening scripture, its evident that the Baptism of the Holy Spirit eventually breath fire on us just as it happened in Acts. (The early Church). We are baptized with fire by God because we are GOLD. In Lamentations 4: 1 the Bible says, "How the gold has lost its luster, the fire gold become dull. The scared gems are scattered at every street corner".
This happens in our lives because we the "Gold" refused to be purged by God. You can never make gold beautiful, nice, elegant if it does not pass through fire. As a Christian, if you have not called the fire of the Lord upon your life, don't call out the fire of the Lord on another persons' life.

WHY DO WE HAVE TO BE BAPTIZED WITH FIRE
  • 1. The Baptism of the Holy Spirit and Fire opens you up to the realm of the Supernatural.
  • 2. It gives you access to signs and wonders.
  • 3. It ushers you into your assignment.
Let the Baptism continue to remain on you to do greater works for God. Amen!
