The Love Congregation welcomes Reverend Dr. Joseph Kofi Aidoo and his family. He is the new Minister of the Congreagtion, taking over from Rev. Mrs. Korkor Hammond.

Induction of the New Session

It is a joint service, starting at 9am. The congregation should be in the official Presby attire or group attire in support of the occasion.

December Declaration

Jeremiah 31:25 says that, _“I will fully satisfy the needs of those who are weary and fully refresh the souls of those who are faint”.
December is our month of Satisfaction and Refreshing. In this month my prayer for you beloved is that, God should;

SATISFY YOU WITH HIS GOODNESS
SATISFY YOU WITH HIS DIVINE PROVISION
SATISFY YOU WITH HEALING AND GOOD HEALTH
SATISFY YOU WITH WHATEVER YOUR HEART DESIRE IS, ACCORDING TO GOD'S PLAN FOR YOUR LIFE
REFRESH YOUR SOUL WITH JOY
REFRESH YOUR SOUL WITH PEACE
REFRESH YOUR SOUL WITH ANYTHING YOUR SOUL YEARNS FOR IN CHRIST JESUS
MAY THE GOD OF ALL GRACE KEEP AND PROTECT YOU AND YOUR FAMILY THIS MONTH.

STAY BLESSED IN JESUS' NAME

AMEN

(You can use these Declarations as Prayer Points during your personal prayer time)
Rev Dr Joseph Kofi Aidoo
