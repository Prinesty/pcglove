The Church is made of seven (7) main departments.
The Departments work with the Committees put in place by the Session in line with the church's constitution.
The Departments are:
Administration & Human Resource Development & Social Services Ecumenical Social Relations Department of Mission & Evangelism Finance Department of Church Life and Nurture Education
Mandate / Functions

Department of Administration and Human Resource Management coordinates the work of the following committees:


1. Committee on Human Resource

2. Committee on Property Management and Logistics
Mandate / Functions

Department of Development and Social Services coordinates the work of the following committees:


1. Scholarship Committee

2. Social and Health Service Committee
Mandate / Functions

The committee is entrusted with the responsibility to advise the Church on matters affecting the relations of the Church with other churches and with Inter-Church or Ecumenical Bodies.


Department of Ecumenical Social Relations coordinates the work of the following committees:


1. Committee on Ecumenism and Relations
Mandate / Functions

Keep under review the whole evangelistic task of the Church and the way it is being carried out.
Encourage and guide the Bible Study and Prayer Group.
Promote the evangelistic endeavours of other groups in the Church


Department of Missions and Evangelism coordinates the work of the following committees:


1. Committee on Evangelism
Mandate / Functions

Department of Finance coordinates the work of the following committees:


1. Welfare Committee

2. Credit Union

3. Harvest Committee

4. Project and Investment Committee
Mandate / Functions

The Department works out a mechanism for providing members of the Church with Christian Education


Department of Church Life and Nurture coordinates the work of the following committees:


1. Church Life and Nurture (CLAN)

2. Committee on Worship

3. Committee on Counselling and Family Life
Mandate / Functions

Responsible for the operations of the School and Education in the Church


Department of Education coordinates the work of the following committees:


1. Education Committee

2. School Management Committee (SMC)
